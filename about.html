About ME

Hello, I am Noran Borg and I am a graphic designer based in Malta. I am studying for a degree for the passion I have which is graphic design. I like visualising ideas and communicating with people through design, this is what makes graphic design valuable for me. Passion. The coursework I have when studying graphic design has equipped me with technical skills and is encouraging me to design with creativity more. I have always had a love for the creative industry, especially paths requiring art and design skills. This summer I got the opportunity to have an apprenticeship as a graphic designer and this opportunity expanded my skills in using the Adobe Creative Cloud and in creativity. My goal is to become a professional graphic designer and I hope to create designs that would travel the world. Other than design I love photography and videography, I like creating social content.
